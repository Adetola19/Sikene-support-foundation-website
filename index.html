Our Mission

A name that carries
a promise.

Sikene — meaning "God's gift" and "good laughter" — is not just a name. It is a declaration. Every child is a blessing. Every community deserves to know joy. And every person who gives is participating in something sacred.

We started Sikene Support Foundation because we believe the greatest things in life aren't meant to be held — they're meant to be passed forward.

🌿

God's Gift

Every person we serve is precious. Every child in an orphanage, every student walking barefoot — their lives matter, and our work says so.

✨

Good Laughter

We measure success not just in numbers but in smiles — in a child running with new shoes, in a family sharing a hot meal, in a community that knows it is seen.

🤝

Gifted to Give

The blessing doesn't end with us. When you give, you become part of a chain that stretches further than you can see. You were made for this.

Partnerships

Build something together.

Corporate partners are the backbone of what Sikene can grow into. Whether through financial support, in-kind donations, or staff volunteering — your organisation can be part of changing lives at scale.

Gold Partner

Lead sponsor of a full program — named recognition on all materials, social features, impact reports

Silver Partner

Co-sponsor of a single drive or event — branded presence, staff volunteering slots, impact summary

In-Kind Partner

Donate goods, logistics, or services — shoes, food, transport, printing — fully tax-deductible

Community Partner

Schools, churches, community groups — join us as a distribution or mobilisation partner
